Copyright © 2022 4share Authors. All rights reserved. Our dream was to utilize technology around us & allow multiple users to download & upload files wirelessly & being offline. With the development of 4share our dream come true. The 4share is designed to keep pace with the changing needs and times. We want to make it easier for you to share things at a brilliant speed.

-> The Apache Tomcat® software.
-> MySQL Databse.
-> Browser like google chrome, Microsoft edge, Mozilla firefox.

-> Start the MySQL & Tomcat®.
-> Login to the 4share.
-> Now share your files.
